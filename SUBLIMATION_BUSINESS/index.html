How It Works

From concept to creation in three simple steps

Production & Delivery

Your order enters our climate-controlled production facility. Each piece undergoes QC inspection before premium packaging and fast shipping.

Turnaround:

  • Standard: 5-7 business days
  • Rush: 2-3 business days
  • Same-day available

Artwork Specifications

To ensure your designs achieve maximum vibrancy and detail, please provide files meeting these professional specifications:

  • Resolution: 300 DPI minimum at actual print size
  • Color Mode: CMYK preferred (RGB accepted)
  • Formats: .AI, .EPS, .PDF (vector) or .PSD, .TIFF (raster)
  • Bleed: Add 0.125" bleed for edge-to-edge designs
  • Fonts: Convert to outlines/curves or include font files

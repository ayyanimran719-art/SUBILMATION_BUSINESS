FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the minimum order quantity?

We have no minimum order quantity! You can order as few as one piece or thousands. However, bulk orders receive better pricing.

How long does production take?

Standard production time is 5-7 business days. Rush services are available for 2-3 business days. Contact us for same-day options.

What file formats do you accept?

We accept AI, EPS, PDF, PSD, PNG, and JPG files. For best results, provide vector files (AI, EPS, PDF) at 300 DPI or higher.

Do you offer design services?

Yes! Our professional design team can help create or optimize your artwork. Free consultations are included with all orders.

What is sublimation printing?

Sublimation is a printing process where heat and pressure turn ink into gas, which bonds with polyester fibers. This creates permanent, vibrant prints that won't crack, peel, or fade.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes, we ship worldwide! Shipping costs and times vary by location. Contact us for an accurate international shipping quote.
